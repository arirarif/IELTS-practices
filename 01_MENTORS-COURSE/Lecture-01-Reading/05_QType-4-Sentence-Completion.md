# 🅳 QUESTION TYPE 4 — Sentence Completion
### Mentor's method + "Events in City Libraries" solved · English content · বাংলা ব্যাখ্যা

---

## 🎯 The mentor's method (৬ ধাপ)
1. **Word-limit গোল করো** (এখানে: *ONE WORD ONLY*)। ২ শব্দ লিখলে ভুল।
2. অসম্পূর্ণ বাক্যটা **পুরো পড়ো** — gap-টা **কোন জাতের শব্দ** চায় বুঝে নাও (noun? verb? নাম?)।
3. বাক্যের **keyword** ধরো (যেটা paraphrase হয় না — নাম, সংখ্যা, technical শব্দ)।
4. Passage-এ scan করে **paraphrase করা জায়গা** খোঁজো।
5. **Grammar মিলিয়ে** শব্দ বসাও — বাক্যটা পড়লে যেন ব্যাকরণসম্মত শোনায়।
6. Sentence-completion সাধারণত **passage-এর ক্রমে** আসে।

> 🔑 **বাংলা মূল কথা:** এখানে gap-এর আগের-পরের শব্দই বলে দেয় কী **form** লাগবে (singular/plural, verb tense)। বসিয়ে বাক্যটা মনে মনে পড়ো — "শুনতে ঠিক লাগছে?" না লাগলে form বদলাও।

---

## 📖 EXERCISE — "Events in City Libraries"
**Instruction:** *ONE WORD ONLY.* (Storytime / After-school centers / Adults' book group — তিনটা অংশ)।

### ✅ ANSWERS
| Q | Answer | Locate (passage paraphrase) |
|---|--------|------------------------------|
| 1 | **authors** | stories read aloud by librarians and sometimes by the **authors** themselves |
| 2 | **languages** | storytimes in **languages** other than English (Mandarin, Maori, Swedish) |
| 3 | **projects** | help with the particular **projects** that their teacher has set |
| 4 | **presentation** | enhance the **presentation** of work submitted… a key aspect of assessment |
| 5 | **crime** | **crime** is next on the list for November/December |
| 6 | **January** | This group does not meet in the month of **January** |

---

### Step-by-step (কীভাবে বের হলো)

**Q1.** "Children are told stories by ___ as well as by librarians."
- gap = মানুষ/দল (noun)। keyword "told stories", "as well as librarians"।
- Locate: *"read aloud by librarians and sometimes by the **authors** themselves."* → **authors** ✓।

**Q2.** "Storytime sessions might be heard in a variety of ___ in different libraries."
- "a variety of ___" → plural noun। keyword "variety"।
- Locate: *"storytimes in **languages** other than English."* → **languages** ✓ (plural form ঠিক)।

**Q3.** "Librarians can assist children with researching their school ___."
- gap = noun (school ___)। keyword "assist… researching"।
- Locate: *"help with the particular **projects** that their teacher has set."* → **projects** ✓।
  - ⚠️ "project" নয় **projects** — passage-এ plural; ONE WORD limit মানা ✓।

**Q4.** "Librarians can help children with the ___ of their work because this is something that will be graded."
- "the ___ of their work" → noun। keyword "graded / will be graded"।
- Locate: *"enhance the **presentation** of work submitted, which is often a key aspect of **assessment**."* (assessment = graded) → **presentation** ✓।

**Q5.** "Book club members are planning to read books on the subject of ___ at their next meeting."
- gap = একটা topic (noun)। keyword "next meeting / planning to read"।
- Locate: *"**crime** is next on the list for November/December."* → **crime** ✓।

**Q6.** "No meetings in ___ have been scheduled for the book club."
- gap = একটা মাস (noun)। keyword "no meetings"।
- Locate: *"This group does not meet in the month of **January**."* → **January** ✓ (বড় হাতের J)।

---

## ⚠️ Sentence-completion ফাঁদ (নম্বর বাঁচাও)
1. **Form ভুল** — "project" বনাম "**projects**"; passage-এর form রাখো, কিন্তু বাক্যের grammar-ও মানো।
2. **Word-limit** — "ONE WORD ONLY" মানে ১ শব্দ; "the month of January" লিখো না, শুধু **January**।
3. **Paraphrase মিস** — "graded" = "assessment", "told stories by" = "read aloud by"। হুবহু শব্দ না খুঁজে **অর্থ** খোঁজো।
4. **বানান/বড়-ছোট হাতের অক্ষর** — মাস/নাম বড় হাতের (January, Mandarin)।

> 🧠 **Takeaway:** Sentence completion = short-answer-এর মতোই, কিন্তু **grammar fit** বেশি গুরুত্বপূর্ণ। বসিয়ে বাক্যটা পড়ো — ব্যাকরণে আটকালে form বদলাও।
