01 · The schedule

Your 8-Week Calendar

15 Jun → ~13 Aug 2026. Six study days + one rest day per week. Pick a time tier and protect the hours like the exam itself.

🔑 The Error Log is your secret weapon One file/notebook. Every mistake → the error, the correct version, the rule. Re-read weekly. You drill your gaps, not generic ones. (Your app's IssueTags + History do this automatically — review them too.)

Week by week

🔍 Week 1 — Diagnose + learn the method

  • Day 1: Set up Cambridge listening audio (QR → phone). Do a full timed baseline mock (Cam 16). Score honestly — it sets your tier + focus.
  • Days 2–6: Learn technique, one skill/day (Simon Reading/Speaking/Writing, one Task 1 chart, listening types).
  • Start the Error Log + daily vocab. Tell the app your real bands.

🛠 Weeks 2–3 — Build technique into habit

  • Daily template every day; rotate skills (~2 sessions each/week).
  • Writing: 3 essays/week (T2 ×2, T1 ×1) → app score → Break It Down → fix the lowest criterion.
  • Reading/Listening: 1 timed section daily; log wrong answers by type.
  • Speaking: record 3×/week; check fluency + Bangla-L1 sounds.
  • End Week 3: half-mock (R+L timed) + get 2 essays checked by a real teacher.

📊 Week 4 — Mid-point full mock + correct course

  • Full timed mock (Cam 17). Compare to baseline. Refocus the second half on your 2 weakest question types + lowest writing criterion.

🔥 Weeks 5–6 — Intensive, exam-conditions

  • 1 full mock/week (Cam 18, then 19) under strict conditions.
  • Daily timed sections in 2 weakest areas + 3 essays/week.
  • Speaking: drill Makkar Speaking Guesswork (May–Aug 2026) — likely your real questions. Record full 2-min Part 2 turns.

🎯 Week 7 — Peak + simulate the real day

  • 2 full mocks (Cam 20 + a spare) in full simulation, same time of day as your real test. Target L7.5 R7.5 W6.5+ S6.5+.

😴 Week 8 — Taper (sleep wins points)

  • Light review of Error Log + best essays + vocab. No new material. Confirm logistics. Sleep well the last 2 nights.
