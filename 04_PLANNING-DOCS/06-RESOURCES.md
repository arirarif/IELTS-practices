## ⚠️ How to USE these (don't drown)

1. **One teaching source per technique.** Watch *one* IELTS Liz video on T/F/NG, then *practice* — don't watch five videos on the same thing. **Watching ≠ studying.**
2. **80% practice, 20% learning.** After Week 2, you should mostly be *doing tests*, not watching lessons.
3. **All mocks from Cambridge books** (real questions). Use YouTube only to learn a *method* you're weak on.
4. **Everything else routes through me** for marking & diagnosis.
