# 🗓️ 08 — THE 70-DAY PROGRAM (Week-by-Week)

> A clean **10-week / 70-day** curriculum. **No fixed calendar** — Day 1 is the day you start; everything counts forward from there. Miss a day? Resume at the next day number. **Always practice timed.**

---

## 🧭 The Repeatable Daily Template (your default day)

| Block | Time | What |
|---|---|---|
| 🔥 Warm-up | 15 min | Spelling drill / vocab review / read aloud |
| 🎯 Main module (rotates) | 60 min | Today's focus — **timed**, real conditions |
| ➕ Second module | 45 min | Secondary focus / weak point |
| 🔍 Review & log | 30 min | Mark mistakes → log patterns in `07-PROGRESS-TRACKER.md` |
| 🗣️ Speaking (daily, non-negotiable) | 15 min | Talk out loud / 1 recorded cue card |

> **Rotation principle:** Reading & Writing get the most reps (hardest to fix). Listening near-daily. Speaking *every* day, even if short.

---

# 🟦 PHASE 1 — FOUNDATION & DIAGNOSIS · Weeks 1–3 (Days 1–21)
> *Learn the game. Find the leaks. Get an honest baseline.*

### Week 1 — Diagnostic & Format (Days 1–7)
| Day | Focus |
|---|---|
| **Day 1** | Read `00` → `01`. Set up resources (`06`). Understand the mission & strategy. |
| **Day 2** | 🧪 **FULL DIAGNOSTIC MOCK** — all 4 modules, strict timing, one sitting. Send me everything. |
| **Day 3** | Review the diagnostic *with me*. Fill baseline in `07`. Read `02-LISTENING`; do 1 listening section. |
| **Day 4** | Read `03-READING`. Do 1 passage, 20-min cap. Diagnose: timing problem or accuracy problem? |
| **Day 5** | Read `04-WRITING`. Write **1 Task 1** → send for marking. |
| **Day 6** | Read `05-SPEAKING`. Record 1 cue card. Do 1 listening section. |
| **Day 7** | ☕ Light: review the week, vocab, **Week-1 check-in** in `07`. Rest. |

### Week 2 — Question-Type Mastery, Part 1 (Days 8–14)
- 🎧 Learn each Listening question type (1 section/day, focus on **distractors + spelling**).
- 📖 Drill **True/False/Not Given** in isolation (the #1 reading band-killer) + 1 passage timed.
- ✍️ Write **1 Task 2** essay → marked + **rewritten** from feedback.
- 🗣️ Daily cue card. Build Part 1 fluency (answer in 2–3 sentences, never one word).
- ✅ End with the **Week-2 check-in** in `07`.

### Week 3 — Question-Type Mastery, Part 2 (Days 15–21)
- 🎧 Map labelling + multiple choice + Section 4 stretch.
- 📖 Drill **Matching Headings** + Matching Information. Enforce the 20-min/passage cap.
- ✍️ 1 Task 1 + 1 Task 2, both marked + rewritten. Start your recurring-error kill-list.
- 🗣️ Daily cue card + first **full speaking mock** (I play examiner).
- 📊 **End-of-Phase-1 review:** re-assess targets vs reality and tune the plan to YOU.

# 🟩 PHASE 2 — STRATEGY & DRILLING · Weeks 4–7 (Days 22–49)
> *Convert knowledge → automatic technique. Hammer weak points. This is where bands are built.*

**Weekly rhythm (repeats each week in Phase 2 — "Day A–G" = the 7 days of that week):**
| | Day A | Day B | Day C | Day D | Day E | Day F | Day G |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Main** | 📖 Reading deep | ✍️ Writing T2 | 🎧 Listening | 📖 Reading deep | ✍️ Writing T1 | 🧪 Half mock | ☕ Review |
| **+** | Listening sec | Reading T/F/NG | Writing rewrite | Listening sec | Reading headings | Speaking mock | Vocab + log |
| **🗣️** | cue card | cue card | cue card | cue card | cue card | full mock | listen-back |

**Per-week emphasis (driven by YOUR tracker — adjust with me each week):**
| Week | Days | Emphasis |
|---|---|---|
| **Week 4** | 22–28 | Reading timing + T/F/NG to instinct. Writing Task 1 **overview** mastery. |
| **Week 5** | 29–35 | Writing Task 2 — all prompt types, position clarity. Listening distractors. |
| **Week 6** | 36–42 | Whichever module is **lagging in the tracker** gets a double-dose week. |
| **Week 7** | 43–49 | Speaking Part 2/3 depth + reading full-test pacing. 1 full mock. |

> 🎯 By end of Week 7: Listening/Reading near target (30+/40), Writing structures automatic.

# 🟥 PHASE 3 — EXAM SIMULATION & POLISH · Weeks 8–10 (Days 50–70)
> *Make the real exam feel boring. Peak — don't burn out.*

| Week | Days | Plan |
|---|---|---|
| **Week 8** | 50–56 | 🧪 **2 full timed mocks** (computer-delivered conditions, typed Writing). Deep error review. 1 marked essay. Daily speaking mock. |
| **Week 9** | 57–63 | 🧪 **2 full mocks** under exact exam timing. Lock templates + vocab bank. Eliminate your top 3 recurring errors. Speaking + Part 3 polish. |
| **Week 10** | 64–70 | 🧪 1 final light mock early. Then **review only — no new material.** Last 2 days: rest, sleep, light review. 📍 **TEST.** You've already "taken it" 6+ times. Perform, don't prove. **Acquire the 7.** |
